Biography

I’m a second-year PhD candidate of Northwestern Polytechnicl University (NWPU), advised by professor Yanning Zhang. I obtained my M.S. and B.S. degree in NWPU in 2016 and 2019.

My research interests lie in 3D scene perception, including depth estimation, 3D modeling and 6-DoF pose estimation.

Projects

Semantic-guided Depth Estimation
In this project, we enhance the self-supervised depth estimation via an auxiliary semantic segmentation task.
Robust Self-supervised Depth Estimation
In this project, we improve the robustness of self-supervised depth estimation via proposing a set of robust constraints that are unified in the final loss.
Efficient and Robust Structure-from-Motion
This project improves the SFM by proposing efficient robust estimation method as well as a robust and accurate hybrid SFM pipeline.
